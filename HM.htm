Calamity Mod: Hardmode Progression Guide

Introduction

The Calamity Mod introduces a plethora of new content to Terraria, including new biomes, enemies, bosses, and items. This guide will help you navigate through the hardmode phase of the game, ensuring you are well-prepared for the challenges ahead.

Guide Reminders

  • This guide gives only lists items that are enough for the boss, no overpreparation or underpreparation
  • Stock up on healing potions and any class related potions
  • The Boss Arenas is completely optional, you may do your own version
  • Some boss are not listed because they're either not required or they're in the same boss guide
  • The list for accessories may contain less than the previous, because it's simply an addition to your previous loadout/the item shouldn't be replaced
  • Bosses that are mentioned in the same box means that they require just the same amount of preparation
