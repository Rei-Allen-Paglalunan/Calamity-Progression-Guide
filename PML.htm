Calamity Mod: Post-Moonlord Progression Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Post-Moon Lord progression guide! After defeating the Moon Lord, you've unlocked access to some of Calamity's most challenging bosses. This guide provides class-specific equipment recommendations for each major progression milestone, helping you prepare effectively for every encounter. Use the tabs below to view equipment for your selected class, and remember that preparation and proper gear are essential for success.

Guide Tips for Post-Moon Lord

  • Preparation is Key: Stock up on healing potions, class-specific potions, and buff accessories
  • Armor Priority: Class-specific armor is recommended for optimal damage output
  • Accessories Matter: Equip all available accessory slots for maximum survivability and damage
  • Expert Difficulty: These bosses are designed for Expert/Revengeance mode - adjust difficulty as needed
  • Arenas Optional: Boss arenas can help, but are not required for victory
  • All-Class Items: Can be used by any class in addition to class-specific items
